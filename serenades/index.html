Questions

Everything you need
to know.

Is this really private — just the two of us?
Yes. Completely. No other guests, no other groups, no strangers. The canyon location is selected for you. Tyler performs exclusively for you for the full concert. The photographer is there only for you. This is not a shared experience.
Are children allowed?
Sedona Serenades is designed exclusively for adult couples. For families or groups with children, Tyler's Red Rock Nature Concert is a wonderful option.
How far in advance should we book?
A minimum of 5 days is required. Peak dates — Valentine's Day, New Year's Eve, fall season — book 4–6 weeks out. If you have a specific date in mind, the sooner you reach out, the better.
What if it rains?
Sedona averages over 300 sunny days per year. In the rare event of weather, Tyler will work with you to reschedule at no additional cost.
How far do we walk?
The primary Sedona Serenades location is a short, easy walk from parking — approximately 5 minutes on a gentle path. If mobility is a consideration, let us know when you inquire and Tyler will select the accessible Cultural Park location.
What should we wear?
Comfortable layers — Sedona evenings shift quickly. Closed-toe shoes for the short walk. You'll look stunning in the photographs regardless of what you wear.
Can we add a bottle of champagne?
Absolutely — bring what you'd like to celebrate with. The canyon doesn't have a corkage fee.
