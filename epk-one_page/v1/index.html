Sedona, Arizona

Tyler Carson

Fiddler on the Rock
CBS Mornings Commonwealth Games · 60,000 Live Victoria Symphony Orchestra Sandy Kelly · Johnny Cash Collaborator Sedona Monthly · Discover the Region

Featured on CBS Mornings and described as one of the most extraordinary live musical experiences in America. A former child prodigy who performed for 60,000 people at the Commonwealth Games, debuted as soloist with the Victoria Symphony Orchestra at thirteen, and toured Ireland with Sandy Kelly — Johnny Cash's collaborator. Tyler calls Sedona home, where the red rock canyons have become both his stage and his inspiration.
