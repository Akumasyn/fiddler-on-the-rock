Tyler Carson

Featured on CBS Mornings and described as one of the most extraordinary live musical experiences in America, Tyler Carson is a violinist known for emotionally powerful performances that blend virtuosity, storytelling, and cinematic atmosphere. A former child prodigy who performed for 60,000 people at the Commonwealth Games, debuted as soloist with the Victoria Symphony Orchestra at age thirteen, and toured Ireland with Sandy Kelly — longtime collaborator of Johnny Cash — Tyler now brings his signature performance style to audiences around the world through his project, Fiddler on the Rock.
