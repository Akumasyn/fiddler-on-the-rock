Internationally Touring Violinist

Tyler Carson

▶  Watch Performance

Tyler Carson is an internationally touring violinist known for delivering powerful, emotionally driven performances across stages worldwide. A prodigy from a young age, he has performed across multiple countries — blending classical mastery with modern expression to create a sound that resonates with diverse audiences. Now returning to the global stage, Tyler brings a unique performance style shaped by world touring, award-winning documentary work, and his signature residency in Sedona's iconic red rock landscapes.

Featured on CBS Mornings — nationally broadcast live performance
Performed live for 60,000 people at the Commonwealth Games
Soloist with the Victoria Symphony Orchestra at age 13
International touring artist — Ireland, India, Canada, and beyond
Collaborated with Sandy Kelly, Johnny Cash's touring partner
Award-winning documentary subject — "Living Music" (multi-festival run)
Featured: Sedona Monthly · Discover the Region · Red Rock News · Verde Valley
